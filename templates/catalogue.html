LOCATIONS

  • Nine Arch Bridge

    The Nine Arches Bridge also called the Bridge in the Sky, is a viaduct bridge in Sri Lanka. It is one of the best examples of colonial-era railway construction.

  • Sigiriya Rock

    Sigiriya or Sinhagiri is an ancient rock fortress located in the northern Matale District near the town of Dambulla in the Central Province, Sri Lanka.

  • Nilaveli Beach

    Nilaveli is a coastal resort town and suburb of the Trincomalee District, Sri Lanka located 16 km northwest of the city of Trincomalee.

  • Ambuluwawa

    Hilltop place of worship with an unusual tower combining architecture styles of many religions. In the central highlands of Sri Lanka.

  • Yala National Park

    Yala was designated as a wildlife sanctuary in 1900, and, along with Wilpattu was one of the first two national parks in Sri Lanka.

  • The Temple of the Tooth Relic

    Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic or Sri Dalada Maligawa; commonly known as the ශ්‍රී දළදා මාළිගාව, is a Buddhist temple in Kandy.
